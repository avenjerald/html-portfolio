The Best Movies According to JC

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Officer Black Belt

Officer Black Belt is a Korean movie about a man who is recruited by a probation officer to help monitor individuals on probation who wear ankle monitors. I personally love it because the main character lives an honest life and always strives to do what is right, no matter the circumstances.

Divergent

I love the idea of factions, just as much as I love the concept of houses in Harry Potter. The plot, especially the idea of people who can't be confined to just one faction—called Divergent—really resonates with me. I feel like I can relate. While we don’t have literal factions in real life, I often feel like I’m capable of doing many things, without belonging to just one particular category.

The Maze Runner

I really love the concept of The Maze Runner. I often find myself wondering what I would do if I were one of them. I'm especially inspired by how courageous many of the characters are.
